NutriSphere is an application that helps users maintain a healthy and fit lifestyle through daily meal plan trackers, as well as providing motivation through its unique system: Spheres

"Health & Fitness Simplified". Maintaining physical fitness is something to be proud of because a healthy body is one that seeks to find methods to support oneself and one's family. In order to achieve daily objectives in a balanced manner, a sound and active mind is necessary.

But because of their incessant need for commodities and their fast-paced lifestyles, people are always on the go and have little time for self-care. As a result, we present to you a health and fitness-care application that enables users to quickly check on and manage their nutritional status and physical condition.

Research & Valuable Insights

We conducted UX surveys and collected multiple responses in order to better understand the problem and create the best solution possible. Most people are aware that they should be eating healthier and do some kind of fitness or physical activity for their physical well-being. Some people do some kind of daily form of exercise, such as walking, running, or workouts. Others lack motivation to do so or just do not have the time to optimally check if they are getting the required nutrients to maintain a healthy body. .

Spheres and the Sphere Space

During our brainstorming and ideation sessions, one major problem came to mind. How would we motivate users to continue to use our app? We have created a virtual space on our application prototype called the Sphere Space. In this space, users can add their own "Spheres", which act as physical representations of a day of the year. Users can create their own meal plan and set their own physical goals and accomplishments, and our nutrition calculator (Which calculates recommended daily calories, carbs, proteins, fats, hydration, and exercise amount) will assign one of three outcomes for the Sphere. As users add more and more Spheres, they start to animate and take form of different unique diagrams that are different for each user.

Clean User Interface

For the finest user interface, a minimalist style was adopted that was nearly imperceptible to the user. A calm and soothing color theme of basil and green was adopted for a relaxing nutrition-related effect on the user's mind. We utilized standard UI components to reduce cognitive load and interface redundancy on the user and allow them to do tasks more quickly.
